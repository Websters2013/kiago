Quality standards for Doctors

Our doctors make up the very core of our communities. You bring a unique medical perspective, expertise and passion, to the field of preventative healthcare and wellness. One main prerequisite in having you on board is the willingness to collaborate with your Kaigo team in order to educate and bring healing to your community. By doing so, you are able to offer members and other health partners an experience you wouldn’t be able to offer independently. To help ensure all doctors meet the community’s expectations, you must meet the standards listed below before members or partners can book Kaigo experiences and appointments with you.

These standards apply to your individual consultations as well as group experiences.

They will also help you earn great member reviews and partner recommendations. You can see how you’re doing by checking feedback from members and partners. Keep in mind that if you consistently receive low ratings or complaints or there are other indications that you do not meet these standards, your practice may be temporarily deactivated or removed from the platform. Please remember that we are here to help so always feel free to reach out to us if you are uncertain about any of these standards.

Standards

Doctors share their knowledge with members and health partners in their community, creating opportunities for all to gain new insights and develop a different viewpoint about healthy living. They’re excited to go out of their way to create a different healthcare experience.

  • Credibility.

    When required, wellness partners must always maintain a license in the State in which they are reside, be passionate about their service or theme of experience and be able to demonstrate a deep knowledge of the subject or a skilled mastery of the activity.

  • Access.

    Wellness partners share their insider knowledge with guests and give them behind-the-scenes access to people, places, or activities that guests can’t find on their own.

  • Participation.

    Experiences tell the host’s story so guests become fully immersed in their world. Throughout the experience, hosts create moments that help guests gain a deeper understanding of the host’s background and viewpoint.

  • Connection.

    Wellness partners are thoughtful about creating environments that feel inclusive and intimate. They get to know the individuals or groups members and help everyone feel a sense of personal connection and community.
