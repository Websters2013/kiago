HOW TO BE A KAIGO DOCTOR

  • Sign up to become a Kaigo doc

    If you feel you meet our quality standards, start by creating your profile. Your profile is a place to showcase your specialty and experience.

    • What’s in a your profile?

      Describe your services, take a nice photo and set your hourly price.

    • Your services

      List the services you provide. Set times you’re available to meet with partners and clients.

    • We’re here to assist

      From helping you build an ideal profile and setting an ideal hourly rate, to coordinating your first client appointments and events, your Personal Health Concierge has got you covered.

  • Community is directed to your profile and reaches out to you

    You’ll get messages from clients and partners

    • Send a message

      Messaging online and via the Kaigo app helps you get to know your community and answer any questions.

    • Plan your appointment or event

      Get started by meeting with a new client for the first time or hosting a wellness event with a partner.

      Kaigo handles all of the payments – you never have to worry about juggling the topics of a patient’s well-being and money. Clients get charged before they arrive for their appointments.

      Some of the ways you can choose to get paid include PayPal, direct deposit, and ACH credit. Your payments are sent to you on a weekly basis.
