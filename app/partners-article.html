Quality standards for Wellness Partners

Wellness partners at Kaigo provide preventative health services and compliment the doctor’s offering. In order to provide services and unique experiences to the community, wellness partners must be willing to collaborate with other local partners on the platform. The primary goal is to promote an environment where personal connections are nurtured through educational and entertaining experiences.

Partners including personal chefs, dietitians, personal trainers, yoga instructors, acupuncturists and any other professional involved in preventative health and wellness services.

Wellness experiences are one-of-a-kind activities that tell the story of the wellness professional’s unique service, perspective and passion, offering members something that random internet searches can’t. To help ensure all experiences meet the community’s expectations, they must meet the standards below before members can book.

These standards apply to your individual consultations as well as group experiences.

These standards can also help you earn great reviews. You can see how you’re doing by checking ratings and feedback from your guests. Keep in mind that if you consistently receive low ratings or there are other indications that you do not meet these standards, your experience and services may be temporarily deactivated or removed from the platform.

Standards

Wellness partners enthusiastically share their passion and local knowledge and skill with the world, creating opportunities for guests to gain new insights and a different viewpoint. They’re excited to go out of their way to create meaningful, memorable moments.

  • Credibility.

    When required, wellness partners must always maintain a license in the State in which they are reside, be passionate about their service or theme of experience and be able to demonstrate a deep knowledge of the subject or a skilled mastery of the activity.

  • Access.

    Wellness partners share their insider knowledge with guests and give them behind-the-scenes access to people, places, or activities that guests can’t find on their own.

  • Participation.

    Experiences tell the host’s story so guests become fully immersed in their world. Throughout the experience, hosts create moments that help guests gain a deeper understanding of the host’s background and viewpoint.

  • Connection.

    Wellness partners are thoughtful about creating environments that feel inclusive and intimate. They get to know the individuals or groups members and help everyone feel a sense of personal connection and community.
