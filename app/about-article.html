KAIGO LIVE WELL. ALWAYS

A radically different approach to living a healthy life.

Kaigo was founded with a defiant spirit and a lofty objective: to dramatically change the way healthcare is experienced while making it easier, more personal and way more fun to live a healthy and happy life.

It’s said that necessity is the mother of invention. For us, this need to change the way healthcare is experienced became imperative when one of our founders went through a pretty rough time when his child was diagnosed with a rare illness and had to take matters in his own hands. Not only was it disheartening to spend hours on end waiting to be seen by a doctor only to be rushed in and out of appointments, it was equally shocking to learn how little time was spent in the current health system on preventative care. Subjects like proper nutrition, sleep, exercise and so many crucial issues that affects a person's long term health and quality of life were absent from the care plans.

That’s why we place a very high value on your overall health and wellness. To us, the phrase health and wellness does not simply refer to a lack of illness and disease, but to a more holistic state of being, where your mental, physical and emotional well being are in complete sync. To achieve this, we’ve assembled an amazing team of doctors, dietitians, personal trainers, chefs and other specialists that matter to your individual health. We encourage them to engage with you through curated wellness experiences in your community so that living a healthy life is enjoyable and doesn’t feel like a burden.

Having a personal chef or private doctor should not be reserved only for the elite athletes and entertainers in the world, so we’ve set out to offer the same access to you.

It turns out to be really quite simple: caring people + great experiences = a healthier and happier you.
